# Housing Market Predictor

Advanced AI-powered housing market analysis and prediction engine for Brisbane and Australia (2026-2050).

## Features

- **Interactive Market Analysis**: Real-time parameter adjustment with location, property type, budget, and investment horizon controls
- **AI-Powered Predictions**: Sophisticated modeling for property value forecasting over 1-25 year periods
- **Risk Assessment**: Comprehensive risk analysis across market, interest rate, liquidity, and regulatory factors
- **Visual Analytics**: Dynamic charts showing price predictions and ROI analysis
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first design with dark theme optimized for data visualization

## Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: Pure HTML5, Tailwind CSS v4, Chart.js
- **Design**: Dark theme with gradient accents and professional data visualization
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first approach with grid layouts
- **Interactive**: Real-time slider updates and dynamic chart rendering

## Market Parameters

- **Locations**: Brisbane (CBD, Inner, Outer), Gold Coast, Sunshine Coast, Sydney, Melbourne
- **Property Types**: Units/Apartments, Houses, Townhouses, Vacant Land
- **Budget Range**: $200k - $2M with real-time slider adjustment
- **Investment Horizon**: 1-25 years with compound growth modeling
- **Risk Profiles**: Conservative, Moderate, Aggressive investment strategies

## Prediction Models

The application uses sophisticated financial modeling including:
- Base annual growth rates with location-specific adjustments
- Compound interest calculations for long-term projections
- Risk-adjusted return calculations
- Market sentiment and economic factor integration

## Usage

1. **Set Parameters**: Choose location, property type, budget, and investment timeline
2. **Adjust Risk**: Select your risk tolerance (Conservative/Moderate/Aggressive)
3. **Generate Prediction**: Click "Generate Prediction" for AI analysis
4. **Review Results**: Analyze projected values, ROI, and annual returns
5. **Risk Assessment**: Review market risk factors and investment considerations
