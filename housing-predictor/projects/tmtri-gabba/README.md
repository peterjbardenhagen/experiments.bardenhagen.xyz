# TM@TRI (ENTRI) — Translational Manufacturing Facility

> **Client**: Translational Research Institute (TRI), Brisbane
> **Architect**: Wilson Architects
> **Contractor**: Built Builders
> **Completion**: Early 2026

## Overview

ENTRI (Excellent National Translational Research Initiative), also referred to as **TM@TRI** (Translational Manufacturing at TRI), is a $100M+ biomedical cGMP manufacturing facility located at the Translational Research Institute in Brisbane's Boggo Road Innovation Junction (BRIJ), adjacent to the Gabba precinct.

This is **Australia's first facility of its kind** — a dedicated scale-up biomanufacturing hub designed to bridge the gap between medical research discovery and clinical production.

## Location

Boggo Road Innovation Junction (BRIJ), Brisbane (Meanjin), Queensland  
*Near the Gabba, Princess Alexandra Hospital, and University of Queensland*

## Facilities

- **cGMP Cleanrooms** — Good Manufacturing Practice certified production suites
- **PC2 Laboratories** — Physical Containment Level 2 wet labs
- **Innovation Spaces** — Co-working, meeting rooms, pitch spaces
- **Quality Control Labs** — Onsite QC for clinical trials
- **Product Capabilities**: Biologics, pDNA, RNA-based therapies, cell therapies

## Electrical & Lighting Scope

Commercial-grade electrical infrastructure for:

- **GMP-compliant cleanrooms** — specialist lighting for classified environments
- **PC2 laboratories** — controlled environment lighting and power
- **Office & innovation spaces** — architectural lighting
- **Atrium & common areas** — feature lighting
- **External building lighting** — facade and site illumination
